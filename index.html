Our Story

A Creative Powerhouse
Born in Ghana

GroXincs Enterprise is a multi-studio creative powerhouse based in Kumasi, Ghana. Founded on the belief that African stories deserve world-class visual language, we bring together animation, motion graphics, film production, graphic design and photography under one enterprise — telling stories that educate, inspire and preserve culture across Ghana and beyond.

From 2D and 3D animations to Virtual Reality experiences and brand campaigns, every project we undertake is driven by a commitment to visual excellence, cultural authenticity, and creative innovation.
